## Part I: The Problem

### Economic Data

- **Global Advertising Expenditure**: WARC Global Ad Spend Report (2024); GroupM Forecast (2024). Global advertising passed $1 trillion in 2024; digital channels account for 72.7%.

- **AI Employment Exposure**: International Monetary Fund Staff Discussion Note (January 2024), "AI Will Transform the Global Economy." 40% of global employment exposed to AI. [IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/blogs/articles/2024/01/14/ai-will-transform-the-global-economy-lets-make-sure-it-benefits-humanity)

- **OECD Employment Outlook (2023)**: Jobs at high risk of automation average 27% across OECD countries.

- **Public Anxiety**: Reuters/Ipsos (2023), 71% of Americans fear AI will permanently reduce jobs. Gallup (2023-2024), 75% believe AI will reduce job opportunities. Pew Research (2025), 73% of AI experts optimistic vs. 23% of public. [Pew](https://www.pewresearch.org/social-trends/2025/02/25/u-s-workers-are-more-worried-than-hopeful-about-future-ai-use-in-the-workplace/)

- **Trust in AI**: Bentley-Gallup Survey, 79% of Americans don't trust companies to use AI responsibly. [Bentley](https://www.bentley.edu/gallup/ai)

### Litigation

- **New York Times v. OpenAI**: Case 1:23-cv-11195, S.D.N.Y. (filed December 27, 2023). Federal judge rejected dismissal motion March 2025.

- **Getty Images v. Stability AI**: UK High Court judgment (November 2025) largely rejected infringement claims.

- **Anthropic Settlement**: $1.5 billion settlement announced September 2025. $3,000 per book for ~500,000 works; requirement to destroy pirated libraries. [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2025/09/05/g-s1-87367/anthropic-authors-settlement-pirated-chatbot-training-material)

### Regulation

- **EU AI Act Article 53(1)(d)**: Requires GPAI providers to publish detailed summaries of training content. Effective August 2025. Penalties up to 15 million or 3% of global revenue.

### Productivity-Wage Gap

- **Economic Policy Institute**: "The Productivity-Pay Gap" (2024). From 1979 to 2023, productivity rose 73.9% while typical worker compensation grew only 17.1%. [EPI](https://www.epi.org/productivity-pay-gap/)

### Social Media and Mental Health

- **U.S. Surgeon General's Advisory** (May 2023): Adolescents spending 3+ hours daily on social media face double the risk of depression/anxiety. Average teen use: 3.5 hours/day. [HHS](https://www.hhs.gov/surgeongeneral/reports-and-publications/youth-mental-health/social-media/index.html)

### Philosophy

- **Hannah Arendt**: *Eichmann in Jerusalem: A Report on the Banality of Evil* (1963).

- **Jacques Ellul**: *The Technological Society* (La Technique, 1954).

- **Guy Standing**: *The Precariat: The New Dangerous Class* (2011).

- **Shoshana Zuboff**: *The Age of Surveillance Capitalism* (2019). Defined surveillance capitalism as "the unilateral claiming of human experience as free raw material for translation into behavioral data." [Harvard Gazette](https://news.harvard.edu/gazette/story/2019/03/harvard-professor-says-surveillance-capitalism-is-undermining-democracy/)

## Part II: The Vision

### Economic Theory

- **Mariana Mazzucato**: *The Value of Everything: Making and Taking in the Global Economy* (2018). "Much of what is passing for value creation is just value extraction in disguise." Winner, 2018 Leontief Prize. [Official Site](https://marianamazzucato.com/books/the-value-of-everything/)

- **Jaron Lanier**: *Who Owns the Future?* (2013). Coined "data dignity"—the principle that what you create belongs to you. [TechTarget](https://www.techtarget.com/searchenterpriseai/definition/data-dignity)

- **Francis Fukuyama**: *Trust: The Social Virtues and the Creation of Prosperity* (1995). Trust as economic infrastructure; high-trust societies outperform low-trust societies. [Simon & Schuster](https://www.simonandschuster.com/books/Trust/Francis-Fukuyama/9780684825250)

### Game Theory

- **Robert Axelrod**: *The Evolution of Cooperation* (1984). Tit-for-tat won both computer tournaments. [Stanford PDF](https://ee.stanford.edu/~hellman/Breakthrough/book/pdfs/axelrod.pdf)

- **Elinor Ostrom**: Nobel Prize in Economics (2009) for analysis of commons governance. *Governing the Commons: The Evolution of Institutions for Collective Action* (1990). Swiss Alps commons example: farmers since 1517. [AEI](https://www.aei.org/articles/elinor-ostrom-and-the-solution-to-the-tragedy-of-the-commons/)

### Mechanism Design

- **Nobel Prize (2007)**: Leonid Hurwicz, Eric Maskin, Roger Myerson for mechanism design theory.

### Open Source

- **Linux Statistics**: 96.3% of top million web servers; 100% of TOP500 supercomputers (since 2017); 92% of cloud VMs; 72.6% of Fortune 500 mission-critical workloads; 75%+ of stock exchanges. [Enterprise Apps Today](https://www.enterpriseappstoday.com/stats/linux-statistics.html); [Gitnux](https://gitnux.org/linux-statistics/)

- **Yochai Benkler**: *The Wealth of Networks: How Social Production Transforms Markets and Freedom* (2006). Coined "commons-based peer production."

## Part III: The Architecture

### Existing Attribution Systems

- **ASCAP/BMI**: Performance Rights Organizations distributing $1B+ annually to songwriters based on verified play data. ASCAP (1914), BMI (1939). [ASCAP](https://www.ascap.com/help/royalties-and-payment)

- **H-Index / Google Scholar**: Academic citation tracking across billions of citations. Affects hiring, grants, tenure. Proposed by Jorge Hirsch (2005). [Google Scholar Metrics](https://scholar.google.com/intl/en/scholar/metrics.html)

### AI Training Data Licensing

- **Shutterstock**: $104M revenue from AI licensing (2023), projecting $250M by 2027. Six-year agreement with OpenAI. [PetaPixel](https://petapixel.com/2024/06/04/shutterstock-made-104-million-licensing-assets-to-ai-devs-last-year/)

- **Reddit/Google**: $60M annually for data licensing. [Various Sources]

### Standards

- **W3C Verifiable Credentials 2.0**: Published as W3C Standard, May 2025. [W3C](https://www.w3.org/press-releases/2025/verifiable-credentials-2-0/)

- **C2PA (Content Provenance)**: Coalition formed 2021 (Adobe, Arm, Intel, Microsoft, Truepic). Adopted by Leica, Nikon, Adobe, OpenAI, BBC News. [C2PA.org](https://c2pa.org/)

- **AT Protocol**: Decentralized identity and social applications. Bluesky: 20M+ users. [atproto.com](https://atproto.com/)

- **Solid Project**: Personal data stores. Founded by Tim Berners-Lee at MIT. Early adopters include BBC, NHS. [solidproject.org](https://solidproject.org/)

## Part IV: The Stance

### Steward-Ownership

- **Purpose Foundation**: 300+ companies. Coined "steward-ownership" in 2017 after studying Bosch, Zeiss models. [purpose-economy.org](https://purpose-economy.org/en/)

- **Denmark Industrial Foundations**: Steward-owned companies represent ~60% of Copenhagen stock market value. 6x higher survival probability after 40 years. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steward-ownership); [We Are Stewards](https://wearestewards.nl/en/stock-market-listed-steward-owned-companies-in-denmark/)

- **Novo Nordisk**: Controlled by Novo Nordisk Foundation (28.1% of shares, 77.1% of voting shares). One of world's most valuable companies. [Novo Nordisk IR](https://www.novonordisk.com/investors/stock-information/share-and-ownership-structure.html)

- **Carlsberg**: Foundation-owned since 1888. Fourth-largest brewery globally.

- **Patagonia / Holdfast Collective**: September 2022, "Earth is now our only shareholder." $4.6M directed to Vjosa River national park in Albania. [Patagonia](https://www.patagonia.com/ownership/)

- **Examples**: Ecosia (search engine), Sharetribe (marketplace platform).

### Mission-Driven Talent Advantage

- **Patagonia Retention**: 4% annual turnover (vs. ~12% retail industry average). 9,000 applicants per open position. [LinkedIn Talent Blog](https://www.linkedin.com/business/talent/blog/talent-acquisition/patagronia-shows-why-putting-your-money-where-your-mouth-is-is-a-great-strategy)

- **Purpose-Aligned Performance**: Organizations with engaged employees outperform competitors by 147% in earnings per share. [Socious](https://socious.io/blog/unlocking-the-power-of-purpose)

### Data Sovereignty Movement

- **MyData Global**: Declaration (2017) translated into 13 languages. Influenced Finnish Government Programme, EU Data Strategy. [mydata.org](https://oldwww.mydata.org/declaration/)

## Methodology Note

Sources are categorized by reliability:

- **Primary**: Original research, official reports, peer-reviewed publications, court filings
- **Secondary**: Reputable journalism, institutional analysis, expert commentary
- **Tertiary**: Aggregated statistics, encyclopedic references

All statistics verified against multiple sources where possible. Publication dates noted; readers should verify current figures for time-sensitive data.
