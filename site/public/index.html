the turn

But architecture can be redesigned.

Provenance now has a price: $3,000 per book, paid out at settlement, half a million times over. Regulators demand attribution. Courts enforce it. And the other path is proven — the free software movement runs 96% of the top million web servers because generosity, given infrastructure, compounds. Extraction has a ceiling. Contribution does not.
