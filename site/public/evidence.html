the receipts

Knowledge should never be free of its history.

So here is ours. Every claim on this site traces to a source. Primary sources are original research, official reports, peer-reviewed work, and court filings; where a figure is time-sensitive, the publication date is noted. If we have something wrong, tell us and we will correct it in the open.

  • Public anxiety — Reuters/Ipsos (2023); Gallup (2023–24); Pew Research (2025) 71% of Americans fear AI will permanently reduce jobs; 73% of AI experts optimistic vs. 23% of the public. source →

Method

Sources are ranked primary (original research, official reports, court filings), secondary (reputable journalism, institutional analysis), and tertiary (aggregated statistics). Statistics are verified against multiple sources where possible. Compiled January 2026; time-sensitive figures should be re-verified against the linked originals.
